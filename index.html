Building an Equitable Future:

Assessing the Impacts of the East Bay BRT Project

  • Report
  • Recommendations

Fall 2019 Transportation Planning Studio

The East Oakland BRT Equity Analysis team consists of four graduate students in City Planning in the Department of City and Regional Planning in the UC Berkeley College of Environmental Design. In partnership with Just Cities, we analyzed the East Oakland East Bay Bus Rapid Transit Project (EBBRT) over the course of several months & presented our findings to stakeholders. This work provides a preliminary analysis of available information and data re­garding the past and current impacts of the EBBRT Project on the people, environment, businesses and homes around International Boulevard (East 14th Street) while providing critical insight into the planning and development process.
